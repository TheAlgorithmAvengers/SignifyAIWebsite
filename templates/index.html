About This Initiative

Signify AI is an AI-based platform created to enable real-time two-way translation between hand-based sign language and English, using only a standard mobile phone.

This technology eliminates the need for specialized equipment or interpreters, and it allows the Deaf community to access work, education, and social environments independently and with dignity.

Our mission is to end the marginalization that non-verbal individuals are subjected to and create a society where inclusivity is a reality and communication is seen as a basic human right rather than a privilege enjoyed by few.
